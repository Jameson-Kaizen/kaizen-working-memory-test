Your Child's Memory Check

This is a quick, playful check of your child's working memory, the mental scratchpad they use to hold and use information for a few seconds. It is behind following directions, mental math, and keeping their place in a task.

How it works:
  • Your child does three short games (about 5 minutes).
  • Sit with them, help them get started, then let them play.
  • Their results come back to you at the end.
