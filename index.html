Working Memory Test

This is a real test of your working memory, the mental "scratchpad" you use to hold and work with information for a few seconds.

  • You'll see a short sequence, one item at a time, then it disappears.
  • Round 1 (Numbers forward): tap the numbers back in the same order.
  • Round 2 (Numbers backward): tap them back in reverse order.
  • Round 3 (Pictures): tap a sequence of pictures back in the same order.
  • Each sequence gets longer until it becomes too hard. Takes about 5 minutes.

For an accurate read: don't write anything down, and find a quiet moment without distractions.

An informal estimate for your own insight, not a clinical or diagnostic assessment.
